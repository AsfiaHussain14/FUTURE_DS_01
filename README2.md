Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

Project Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing campaign dataset to evaluate customer subscription behavior and marketing campaign performance. The objective is to identify conversion trends, customer demographics, and opportunities to improve marketing effectiveness.

Dataset

- Dataset: Bank Marketing Dataset
- Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository
- Records Analyzed: 41,188 customers

Tools Used

- Power BI
- Data Visualization
- Marketing Funnel Analysis

Dashboard Components

1. Total Customers
2. Subscription Distribution
3. Customers by Job
4. Customers by Marital Status
5. Customers by Education
6. Conversion Rate (%)

Key Findings

- Total customers analyzed: 41,188.
- Overall conversion rate: 11.27%.
- Most customers did not subscribe to the term deposit.
- Married customers represent the largest customer segment.
- Administrative, blue-collar, and technician occupations contribute the highest customer counts.
- University and high-school educated customers form a significant portion of the customer base.

Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on customer segments with higher conversion potential.
- Improve customer targeting and personalization strategies.
- Design tailored campaigns for different job and education categories.
- Increase engagement and follow-up activities for customers who did not subscribe.

Conclusion

The analysis provides valuable insights into customer behavior and campaign performance. By leveraging demographic and subscription patterns, marketing teams can optimize campaign strategies and improve future conversion rates.
