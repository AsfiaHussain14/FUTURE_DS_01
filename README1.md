# Online Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

This project analyzes online retail sales data using Power BI.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- CSV Dataset

## Analysis Done
- Total Revenue
- Top Products by Revenue
- Country-wise Revenue
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- High-value Customers

## Insights
- United Kingdom has the highest revenue.
- Top products contribute major sales.
- Monthly trend shows sales performance over time.

## Recommendations
- Focus on top-selling products.
- Target high-value customers.
- Improve sales in low-performing countries.
